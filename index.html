COVID-19 Info/Guidelines

COVID-19 (also known as novel coronavirus) appeared back in January from Wuhan China, and has been rapidly spreading across the world ever since.

Simple ways to stay safe

Handwashing is very essential to prevention of the virus. If theres anything you should take away from this, it's proper handwashing! Wash your hands for at least 20 seconds for effective decontamination. Lather your hands thouroughly with soap before rinsing and make sure not to recontaminate your hands while shutting off the tap.


Handwashing video from the WHO

Stay away from anyone not in your household if possible, and keep at least a 2 meter distance if you can. This virus can primarily spread from droplets, but these droplets can also be somewhat aresolized and can hang in the air for a bit. Stay home and only go out if necessary

The virus can be active on surfaces for up to 3 days (depending on the type of surface). Disinfect and clean commonly used surfaces with soap/water, alchool or other products proven to be effective againts COVID-19. Do not touch your face! If you had any virus antigens on your hands, you would be letting them into your body when you touch your eyes, mouth or nose.



















Physical distancing instead of Social isolating

Physical distancing does not have to be social isolation. With all the technology at our disposale, there are many ways to keep in touch with friends and loved ones who you may not be able to see for a while. Stay in touch, and take care of your mental and physical well being!

Perservere! We will get through this
