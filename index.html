COVID-19 Info/Guidelines

COVID-19 (also known as the novel coronavirus or nCoV-2019) appeared back in January from Wuhan, China. Currently, it has been rapidly spreading across the world.

Since we are in a quarantine situation, what measures can we take?

Simple ways to stay safe

(Click buttons for more info)

Hand washing is very essential to prevention of the virus. If there's anything you should take away from this, its proper handwashing! Wash your hands for at least 20 seconds for effective decontamination. Lather your hands thoroughly with soap before rinsing and make sure not to recontaminate your hands while shutting off the tap.


Hand washing video from the W.H.O

Stay away from anyone not in your household if possible, and keep at least a 2 meter distance if you can. This virus can primarily spread from droplets, but these droplets can also be somewhat aerosolized and can hang in the air for a bit. Stay home and only go out if necessary.

The virus can be active on surfaces for up to 3 days (depending on the type of surface). Disinfect and clean commonly used surfaces with soap & water, alcohol or other products proven by offical experts to be effective against COVID-19. Do not touch your face! If you had any viruses on your hands, you would be giving them a pathway into your body when you touch your eyes, mouth or nose.



















Physical Distancing Instead of Social Isolation

Physical distancing does not have to be social isolation. With all the technology at our disposal, there are many ways to keep in touch with friends and loved ones who you may not be able to see for a while. Stay in touch, and take care of your mental and physical well being!

Perservere! We will get through this!
